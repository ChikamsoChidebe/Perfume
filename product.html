About Midnight Elegance

Midnight Elegance is more than just a fragrance—it's an experience that transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary. Crafted by our master perfumers using the finest ingredients sourced from around the world, this sophisticated scent tells a story of mystery, allure, and timeless elegance.

The Inspiration

Inspired by the enchanting beauty of a moonlit garden, Midnight Elegance captures the essence of a romantic evening where shadows dance and secrets whisper. The fragrance opens with the intoxicating allure of black orchid, a rare and precious flower that blooms only in darkness.

The Experience

As the fragrance develops, warm vanilla emerges, wrapping you in a comforting embrace that speaks of luxury and sophistication. The journey concludes with the grounding presence of sandalwood, creating a lasting impression that lingers long after you've left the room.

Premium Ingredients

Top Notes

  • Black Orchid Extract: Rare and exotic, sourced from sustainable farms in Thailand
  • Bergamot Oil: Fresh citrus from the hills of Calabria, Italy

Heart Notes

  • Madagascar Vanilla: Pure vanilla absolute from premium beans
  • Bulgarian Rose: Delicate rose petals harvested at dawn
  • Jasmine Sambac: Night-blooming jasmine from India

Base Notes

  • Mysore Sandalwood: Creamy, sustainable sandalwood from India
  • White Musk: Clean, synthetic musk for longevity
  • Amber Resin: Warm, golden amber from ancient trees
