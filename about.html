Our Story

Crafting exceptional fragrances since 2020

Our Beginning

A Passion for Perfection

Amazing Aura Perfumes was born from a simple yet profound belief: that fragrance has the power to transform moments into memories, and memories into lasting impressions. Founded in 2020 by master perfumer Elena Rodriguez, our journey began in a small atelier in the heart of Grasse, France.

What started as a personal quest to create the perfect scent has evolved into a luxury fragrance house that celebrates individuality, craftsmanship, and the art of storytelling through scent. Each bottle in our collection represents countless hours of meticulous blending, testing, and refinement.
